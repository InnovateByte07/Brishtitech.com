What is Artificial Intelligence?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the simulation of human intelligence in the machines that can think , learn , and make decisions .

Applications of AI

  • Machine Learning
  • Natural Language Processing
  • Computer Vision
  • Robotics

Future of AI

AI is expected to revolutionize industries, from healthcare to finance , making processes more efficient and innovative.
